About BSTE

Raising confidence in technical education.

The Board of Science & Technology Education is a national platform focused on making technical qualifications more reliable, comparable, and useful. We work with institutes, educators, and industry to strengthen skills learning from programme design through credential award.

Our vision

A future where fair, consistent technical assessment creates meaningful opportunity for every capable learner.

Our mission

To uphold quality in technical qualifications through robust standards, sound assessment practice, and practical collaboration.
